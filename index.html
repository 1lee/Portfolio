About Lee

 

Lee is always in search of the sweet spot where ideas, the canvas, and the toolbox converge. He spends all his time cultivating and becoming more proficient at his craft, because he knows that a big idea is only good until the next big idea. He believes that quality is at the root of value and so to him, nothing less than quality is acceptable. His breath of life is to DELIVER, he breathes in high expectations, and breathes out satisfaction. Lee believes that opportunity is the gem in the geode, but also understands that behind every opportunity is a need that must be fulfilled. Go to him wth your needs, he's here to help.

 

Lee is multitalented with an artistic and musical background that offers a unique approach to projects. He's spent significant time on both sides of the management / employee divide and so understands business needs intimately. When he's not webbing, he's chasing or being chased by his toddlers and/or wife. ☻
